# Guitar Hero Interactions

I enjoy playing rhythm games in my free time. One of my all time favorites is Guitar Hero III: Legends of Rock for the Wii, but I also enjoy the other games in the series. What sets Guitar Hero apart is the custom controller that you use to play: It is shaped like a guitar, has 5 buttons that act as frets, a strum bar, and a few other miscellaneous buttons. The controller makes it feel like you're actually playing the music in each level.
